Kernel Information

Gaussian Blur: Smooths signal by averaging neighboring values with Gaussian weights.

Convolution Basics

Convolution combines two signals to produce a third signal that represents how one signal modifies the other.

The kernel (filter) is applied to each point in the signal, computing a weighted sum of neighboring values.

y[n] = Σ h[k] * x[n-k]
